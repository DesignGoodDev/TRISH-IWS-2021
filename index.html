Trish Track

Welcome HRP IWS 2021 Attendees!

As NASA HRP's partner for disruptive, deep space health technologies, the Translational Research Institute for Space Health (TRISH) funds forward-looking approaches with the potential to become game-changers on exploration class missions.

The TRISH Track at the HRP IWS is a series of sessions that highlight the novel science and technologies of TRISH researchers.

Below you'll find a collection of pre-recorded scientific presentations. Join the TRISH track sessions during the virtual IWS to participate in live discussion and Q&A.
